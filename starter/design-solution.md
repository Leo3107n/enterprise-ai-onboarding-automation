AI-Powered Employee Onboarding Automation System
1. Executive Summary

Enterprise onboarding often requires coordination between HR, IT, Compliance, and Hiring Managers. Manual onboarding introduces delays, inconsistent employee experiences, and operational overhead.

This solution uses workflow automation and Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate employee onboarding, validate submitted information, generate onboarding plans, and coordinate onboarding activities across departments.

2. Step-by-Step Workflow Logic
Step 1: Employee Intake

New employee submits:

Personal information
Job information
Required documents

via:

HR Portal
Google Form
HRIS
Step 2: AI Document Processing

AI extracts:

Employee Name
Job Title
Department
Start Date
Employment Type

from:

CNIC/ID
Employment Contract
Tax Forms
Step 3: Validation

System verifies:

Missing documents
Invalid dates
Missing signatures
Incorrect formats

Incomplete submissions are routed to HR.

Step 4: Profile Enrichment

System combines:

Employee data
Department
Location
Manager
Role

to create a complete onboarding profile.

Step 5: Task Generation

Generate tasks for:

HR:

Orientation

IT:

Email account
Laptop
VPN

Manager:

Team introduction
Training assignment
Step 6: Personalized Onboarding Plan

AI generates:

Day 1 Plan
Week 1 Plan
First 30 Days
Step 7: Communication

Generate:

Welcome Email
Manager Brief
IT Setup Instructions
Step 8: Feedback Collection

Collect:

Day 7 Survey
Day 30 Survey

Track onboarding completion.
